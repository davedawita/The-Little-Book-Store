My name is Dawit. I live in Virginia, VA. I developed this app to aid a bibliophile who wants to search books with genre and select a book from a book list to get basic information.


The Little Bookstore was developed in February, 2024. The app lists 9 book genres. Each book genre has a list of books. Each book again will have a summary for the users to read. Users will also have the option of being directed to amazon to buy a book of their preference. The app also includes a book reviews tab that links to a book review site.
